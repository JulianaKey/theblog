📍 Welcome to ZÜ Club

  • Location: Central Ulaanbaatar
  • Music Genre: House, Techno, EDM, R&B, Hip-Hop
  • Entry Fee: Standard & VIP Pricing Available
  • Hours: Open 10PM – Late
  • Best For: High-energy clubbers, dance music lovers, and social nightlife seekers
  • Dress Code: Trendy casual; stylish but comfortable

ZÜ Club is the one club that best embodies Ulaanbaatar's raw nightlife. If you’re searching for high-energy beats, immersive lighting, and a crowd that actually knows how to party, ZÜ is where you’ll find it. Tucked away in the heart of the city, it’s more than just a club—it’s an adventure.

From the moment you enter, the sound takes over. If you thrive on bass-heavy house, pulsing techno, or high-energy hip-hop, the music seems to vibrate through the walls, wrapping you in the energy of the crowd. The lineup is always changing, bringing in both local and international DJs, making every night a completely new experience.

💡 What Makes ZÜ Unique?

  • ✔ Expansive dance floor & LED-lit atmosphere — Lose yourself in the energy
  • ✔ Diverse music selection — Deep house rave one night, hip-hop and R&B party the next
  • ✔ A mix of locals, expats & travelers — A social, electric atmosphere where strangers become friends

ZÜ is home to some of the best electronic music in the city. Whether you're a fan of deep house, techno, or mainstream club anthems, you’ll find yourself moving to the beats that seem to take over the entire space.

Resident & Guest Performers:

  • Local Mongolian DJs bringing a mix of house, techno, and EDM.
  • Mongolian vocalists and performers who occasionally take the stage, adding a live element to the electronic scene.
  • International guest DJs who bring a global club vibe to UB.
  • Special nights featuring hip-hop & R&B takeovers.

What I love most about ZÜ is that the DJs and performers know how to read the crowd. It’s not just about a playlist—it’s a performance. One moment you’re immersed in a hypnotic techno groove, and the next, a Mongolian vocalist steps in with a soulful hook that electrifies the room. The combination of DJ sets with live vocals makes the night feel more like an experience than just a party.

💡 Pro Tip: The best sets usually happen after 1 AM—stay late if you want to experience the night at its peak.

ZÜ is about high-energy, not exclusivity. Whether you’re grabbing a signature cocktail or going all out with bottle service, the club offers something for every vibe.

  • ✔ Cocktails & premium spirits
  • ✔ VIP bottle service available
  • ✔ Prices: Cocktails from 30,000 MNT+ ($8-10 USD), bottles from 250,000 MNT+ ($70+ USD)

The Social Scene

The crowd at ZÜ is a mix of locals, travelers, and expats—people who actually come to dance. The atmosphere is always shifting, making every night feel different.

  • ✔ A crowd that moves—no standing in the corner
  • ✔ Easy to meet new people, solo or with a group

🎟 Cover & Dress Code

  • ✔ Entry: 30,000–50,000 MNT ($8–$14 USD), varies by event
  • ✔ Dress Code: Trendy & stylish—dress like you’re ready to be seen
