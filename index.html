Welcome to UB’s Nightlife Scene!

If you’re looking for the best of Ulaanbaatar’s nightlife, you’re in the right place. UB’s club scene is fast-paced, high-energy, and constantly evolving—but only if you know where to go.

Since my first year of college, I’ve spent every summer in Mongolia, exploring the city’s nightlife—from underground electronic raves to high-end VIP experiences. Whether it’s dancing until sunrise, discovering the best DJs, or partying with a crowd that knows how to have a good time, I’ve experienced it all.

This guide isn’t just a list of clubs; it’s based on firsthand experience. Whether you’re looking for a packed dance floor, an exclusive VIP section, or a club that brings the best energy, I’ll take you inside the most exciting spots in UB.

From the high-energy beats of ZÜ to the exclusive, luxury feel of VAULT and the nonstop party scene of CANTE, this guide will help you experience Ulaanbaatar’s nightlife at its best.

If you’re a traveler, an expat, or just someone who loves a great night out, this guide is for you.
